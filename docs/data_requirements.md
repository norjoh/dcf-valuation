# Data Requirements

## Required Income Statement Fields
- Revenue or Total Revenue
- EBIT, Ebit, or Operating Income

## Required Cash Flow Fields
- Free Cash Flow
OR
- Operating Cash Flow + CapEx

## Required Metadata
- Sector
- Industry
- Currency

## Disallowed Companies
- Banks
- Insurance companies
- Pure financial institutions

## Data Quality Rules
- At least 3 full years of usable history
- Revenue must be non-negative
- FCF volatility flagged
- Years dropped must be reported

## Data Sources
- Yahoo Finance (primary)
- User-uploaded reports (PDF)
